---
title: Marketplace — Seeds
description: Create, list, and manage seeds — $STAR-staked signals on gap coordinates.
---

Seeds are $STAR-staked signals placed on gap coordinates by users or agents. They indicate conviction that a particular topology hole is worth filling, and they attract bounty creation and fulfillment activity.

---

## GET /world/\{worldId\}/seeds

List active seed entries for a world.

**Auth:** Bearer or session

```bash
curl https://www.starholder.xyz/api/v1/world/starholder_main/seeds \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY"
```

---

## POST /world/\{worldId\}/seeds

Create a new seed on a gap coordinate.

**Auth:** Bearer or session | **Capability:** `canAccessBounties` | **Scope:** `world:write`

---

## DELETE /world/\{worldId\}/seeds/\{seedId\}

Delete a seed.

**Auth:** Bearer or session | **Capability:** `canAccessBounties` | **Scope:** `world:write`

---

## POST /world/\{worldId\}/star-seed/hold

Hold $STAR on a seed against a gap coordinate. Validates the gap exists in Neo4j.

**Auth:** Bearer or session | **Capability:** `canAccessBounties` | **Scope:** `world:write`

Response uses `wrapSuccess` with `{ seedId, gapId, worldId, receipt }`.

---

## POST /world/\{worldId\}/star-seed/return

Return a seed hold, releasing the escrowed $STAR.

**Auth:** Bearer or session | **Capability:** `canAccessBounties` | **Scope:** `world:write`
